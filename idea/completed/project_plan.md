# askDrugTox Project Plan - Advanced Intelligence Roadmap

## Phase 1 & 2: Infrastructure & Foundation (Completed)
*   **Loader & Schema:** Functional with `is_historical` and multi-organ support.
*   **Dashboard & Explorer:** Centralized, scientific UI with Pie Chart analytics.
*   **Lineage & Market:** Tracing by generic agent and manufacturer.

## Phase 3: Advanced Market & Regulatory Discrepancy (Current)
**Goal:** Detect and analyze "Safety Disagreements" and "Labeling Lags" across the pharmaceutical market.

*   **Discrepancy Detection Engine:**
    *   **Severity Gaps:** Identify generic agents with conflicting toxicity classifications (e.g., "Most" vs "No").
    *   **Market Outliers:** Flag manufacturers whose safety profiles deviate significantly from the market consensus.
*   **Regulatory Velocity Analytics:**
    *   **Update Latency:** Measure the time delta between the first and last manufacturer to update a specific safety label.
    *   **Market Adoption Tracking:** Visualize how a toxicity class change "spreads" through the market over time.
*   **Evidence & Semantic Audit:**
    *   **Evidence Disparity:** Compare the strength of evidence (Clinical Trial vs Post-marketing) between competitors.
    *   **Omission Detection:** Use AI summaries to identify manufacturers missing specific clinical biomarkers or warnings found in peer labels.

## Phase 4: Intelligence Reporting & Export (Future)
*   **"Controversial Drug" Reports:** Automated PDF dossiers for agents with high market variance.
*   **Company Safety Scorecards:** Benchmarking manufacturers based on their update velocity and classification accuracy.
*   **Batch Market Export:** Export all manufacturer data for a specific generic agent for regulatory review.
